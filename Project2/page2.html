Movie: Under Paris

Plot

After a devastating shark attack kills her husband Chris, marine biologist Sophia joins forces with police diver Adil to stop a giant shark, Lilith, terrorizing Paris. As the navigate the city's underwater tunnels and sewers, they uncover a dark secret behind the shark's presence.

The film has some ecological messaging and commentary on political games that cost lives.
